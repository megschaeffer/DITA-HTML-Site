Zoom Client for Meetings Desktop Application

Logging in from the Zoom desktop application gives you access to more application settings and allows more convenient access to other certain features.

Through the desktop application, you can adjust your camera settings, audio settings, or dual monitor settings; add virtual backgrounds; and test your audio and video before a meeting. It also gives you more convenient access to the following Zoom functions:
  • start an instant meeting
  • start an upcoming scheduled meeting or webinar
  • play any local meeting or webinar recordings that are stored on your computer

Login to your UMN Zoom account using the Zoom desktop application with the following steps:

  1. Download and install the Zoom for Meetings Desktop Application. It may be referred to as Zoom Client for Meetings
  2. Launch the Zoom.us application
  3. Click the Sign In button
  4. Click the Sign In with SSO button on the right

  5. Enter your UMN email address or the umn domain if prompted
  6. Click Continue to open the Zoom UMN Single Sign On page in your default web browser.
    If you are already signed in through your browser, it will automatically log you in.
    If you are not already logged in, enter your Internet ID and password you use for other University of Minnesota logins and click Sign In.Authenticate with Duo if prompted.
  7. If prompted, click Open Zoom.us in the pop-up box that appears. This will take you back to the Zoom for Meetings desktop application
    You will be directed to the Home tab within the Zoom desktop application. From here, you can start a new instant meeting, schedule a meeting, join a meeting, or screen share.

    You can also click the gear icon to access the desktop client settings, such as video and audio settings.
