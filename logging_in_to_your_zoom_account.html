Logging In to Your Zoom Account

Zoom is a video conferencing platform that the University of Minnesota uses to conduct online, synchronous classes. Every UMN student has an account associated with their x500 to login to Zoom.

For security purposes, Zoom logs you out after 120 minutes of inactivity. However, many class meetings now require authentication with your University account to prevent disruptions, so it is important to know how to login to your UMN account. There are three main ways to login to your UMN Zoom account:
  • UMN Zoom website (Web browser)
  • Zoom Client for meetings (Desktop app)
  • Zoom mobile app (Smartphone)
