Table 1. Glossary of Terms
Term Description
Duo Authentication Duo Security is the University's way of adding another layer of security to the information you access online. It is required for all students, faculty, staff, sponsored, and Person-of-Interest accounts.
Domain A domain name is similar to a physical address in that it directs you to a specific website. The University of Minnesota's domain name is umn.
Student ID Your student ID refers to your x500 which is typically the first five letters of your last name followed by three numbers. It is the same username that you use to login to your UMN email account and MyU.
SSO Single sign-on (SSO) is an authentication method that enables users to securely authenticate with multiple applications and websites by using just one set of credentials. For Zoom, it allows you to login using the University's credentials.
Zoom Meeting A Zoom meeting is what you will typically be hosting and attending. It is designed to be a collaborative event with all participants being able to screen share, turn on their video and audio, and see who else is in attendance.
Zoom Webinar Zoom webinars are designed so that the host and any designated panelists can share their video, audio and screen. Webinars allow view-only attendees.
