UMN Zoom Website

Logging in from the UMN Zoom website is the most comprehensive option as it gives you access to features that are unavailable on the desktop and mobile apps.

The UMN Zoom website gives you the ability to
  • schedule a webinar
  • adjust your default meeting or webinar settings
  • access and manage your Zoom cloud recordings
  • adjust your profile settings such as your Personal Meeting ID, Personal Link, Photo, and Host Key
Login to your UMN Zoom account on the UMN Zoom website with the following steps:
  1. Visit the website umn.zoom.us
  2. Click the LOG IN button

    This will take you to the University of Minnesota Single Sign On (SSO) page.
  3. Enter the Internet ID and password that you use for other University of Minnesota logins
  4. Click Sign In and authenticate with Duo if prompted
    This will take you to your Meetings page where you can start an upcoming scheduled meeting or schedule a new meeting.
    Once here, you can also navigate to other pages including Recordings, Webinars, Meeting Settings, and Profile.
